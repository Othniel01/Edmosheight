Our Story

In 1995, a group of three friends, Emily, Mo, and Sam, had a vision of creating a unique hotel experience. They wanted to create a place where guests could feel like they were staying with friends rather than at a sterile hotel. With their combined backgrounds in hospitality, design, and marketing, they pooled their resources and set out to create Edmos - a hotel that would be unlike any other. The trio found a rundown building in a trendy neighborhood, and with a lot of hard work and creativity, transformed it into a cozy and welcoming space. They chose funky decor, like vintage posters and mismatched furniture, to give the hotel a quirky personality.

New York

Edmos New York is located in the heart of Manhattan's trendy SoHo neighborhood, known for its vibrant art scene, stylish boutiques, and eclectic dining options. The hotel is just a few blocks from iconic landmarks like Washington Square Park and the Brooklyn Bridge, making it the perfect home base for exploring all that the city has to offer. The surrounding streets are lined with historic buildings, art galleries, and trendy cafes, giving the area a lively and bohemian vibe. Whether you're a first-time visitor to New York or a seasoned traveler, Emos' prime location is sure to inspire you to discover the city's hidden gems.
